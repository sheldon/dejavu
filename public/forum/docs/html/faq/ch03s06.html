3.6.  How do I stop spammers from signing up and posting on my forum?

From Phorum 5.2 onwards, the comprehensive Spam Hurdles module is included, which includes a number of spam prevention tools, including CAPTCHA (Completely Automated Public Turing test to tell Computers and Humans Apart). For more information on this and other modules, have a look at the Administrator Manual.
